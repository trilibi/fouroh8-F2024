    Low and Slow

    poseted by BGB, November 15, 2012

    This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking you submerge the food (usually vaccum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature of the food. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as "ultra-low-temperature poaching".

    Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before November 30.
