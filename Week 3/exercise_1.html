Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and itâ€™s always a big hit at parties. My father recommends:

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just to being it up to room tempurature before you serve it. In the witnere, try serving it warm."

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a good processor. Blend until as smooth as possible.
  2. Add capers and olive. Pluse the motor a few times until are incorporated, but still retain some texture
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (Optional)
